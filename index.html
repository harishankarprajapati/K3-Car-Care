Frequently Asked Questions

What is car detailing?
Car detailing is a deep cleaning and restoration process for your vehicle. It removes dirt, stains, scratches, and oxidation to make your car look new.
How often should I get my car detailed?
Usually every 4–6 months depending on usage and environment.
What’s the difference between a car wash and car detailing?
Car wash cleans the surface, detailing restores and protects.
Do you use safe and eco-friendly products?
Yes, all our products are safe and eco-friendly.
How long does a detailing session take?
Usually 2–5 hours depending on the service.
